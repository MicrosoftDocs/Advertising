# Psychics, tarot reading, and mediums

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to all ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 22, 2026.

Ads must be compliant with [Microsoft Advertising Misleading Content Policies](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedInformation.md). All claims must be presented as entertainment, lifestyle, or spiritual guidance.

Ads must not:

- Claim to diagnose, cure, or treat medical conditions.
- Promise to solve financial, legal, or relationship issues with guaranteed outcomes.
- Use fear-based tactics to pressure users into action.
- Make unverifiable or exaggerated claims.
