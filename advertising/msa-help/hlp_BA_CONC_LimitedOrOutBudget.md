# What can I do to change Eligible (limited) or Out of Budget delivery status?

The delivery status tells you if your ads are available to display. The following messages indicate your ads might not display as often as they could:

- **Eligible (limited)**. Your ads are running, but to avoid going over your daily budget, Microsoft Advertising occasionally limits the number of times your ad is shown. If your campaign has this status, it can still be successful. But if you increase your budget, your ads might show more often. This status means that your campaign ran out of budget at least once in the past seven days or that we've determined that your campaign will run out of budget in the coming weeks.
- **Campaign out of budget**. No ads are running for this campaign, ad group, ad, or keyword because your campaign budget has been depleted.

## What you can do

To resume your ads or avoid going over your budget, you can increase your budget:

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Campaigns** and select **Campaigns**.
1. In the **Campaigns** table, find the campaign that has the delivery status of *Campaign out of budget* or *Eligible (limited)*.
1. Select the budget for the campaign and then enter a new budget amount.
1. Select **Save**.

For daily budgets, if you see either of these statuses in the Delivery column on the Campaigns page, you can also select the ellipsis icon ![More information icon](media/BA_ScreenCap_DeliveryDetails.png) next to it (or hover over the status). Then, Microsoft Advertising will suggest a few different daily budgets that might increase your ad exposure. If you want to see a larger list of budget suggestions, you can select **See more suggestions** and open Recommendations to **Increase your campaign budgets to show your ads more frequently**.
