# People finder and research sites

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to all ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 8, 2026.

- **Display/Native/Audience**

  - Advertising for services that offer access to background checks, arrest records, mugshots (including removal services), or criminal history reports is prohibited.
  - Advertising for people finder and lookup services is prohibited, unless the service is a social networking platform where users voluntarily create and manage their own public profiles.

- **Search**

  Advertising for services that provide either background checks, arrest records, access, or removal of mug shots and/or criminal backgrounds, and people finder sites is prohibited in the following markets:
