# About the campaign diagnostics tile

The diagnostics tile displays live statuses across key areas, so you can quickly monitor and address issues as they happen. The following tables provide detailed information to help you better understand each status:

## FAQ

- **Account status**

  | Status | Meaning | What to do |
  | --- | --- | --- |
  | **Your account has a financial hold, so you can't run campaigns.** | A financial hold is blocking your campaign delivery. | [Contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) to resolve the financial hold and restore delivery. |
  | **We're reviewing your account verification. Please wait while we finish.** | We're checking to make sure your account has the appropriate verifications to run ads. | No action required while verification is in progress. |
  | **We've detected an issue with your account that requires attention.** | There's an issue that's blocking delivery. | [Contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) to fix the issue and restore account activity. |
  | **Your account is on hold because you haven't completed advertiser identity verification yet.** | Microsoft Advertising requires advertiser identity verification to ensure that customers see ads from trusted sources to improve their overall experience. | Complete advertiser identity verification in your account settings. See [this help article](hlp_BA_CONC_AdvertiserIdentityVerification.md) for instructions. |
  | **We're screening your account for legal and policy compliance. Please wait while we finish.** | We're ensuring that your account meets legal and policy compliance to run ads. | No action required while screening is in progress. |
  | **Your account didn't pass trade screening, so ads aren't serving.** | We found an issue in our trade screening review that's blocking your delivery until it's resolved. | [Contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) to resolve the issue. |
  | **Your account is on hold because it may require government authorization.** | You need required authorization to run ads. | [Contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) to resolve the issue. |
  | **Onboarding complete. Time to create your first campaign.** | Create a campaign to start running ads. | In Microsoft Advertsing, from the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**. You can also follow the instructions in [this help article](hlp_BA_CONC_adcenter_flow.md) to create your first campaign. |
  | **You haven't added a [VAT ID](hlp_BA_CONC_TaxVATInfo.md) yet, so your account is on hold.** | A VAT ID is required to complete account onboarding. | From your manager account, add your VAT ID in the **Settings** > **Manager account settings** page. |
  | **You haven't added your business location yet, so your account is on hold.** | Your [business location](hlp_BA_CONC_EuropaFAQ.md) is required to complete account onboarding. | From your manager account, add your business location in the **Settings** > **Manager account settings** page. |

  | Status | Meaning | What to do |
  | --- | --- | --- |
  | **You don't have an active insertion order, so your account is on hold.** | If you have monthly invoice billing set up for your account, you need to have an active insertion order for your ads to be eligible for delivery.   | **Your account has no available funds, so it's on hold.** | If you have a [prepay account](hlp_BA_PROC_AddFunds.md), you need to add funds to your account before your ads can serve. | Follow the steps in [this help article](hlp_BA_PROC_AddFunds.md) to add funds to your account. |
  | **You haven't added a payment method yet, so your account is on hold.** | You need to set up a form of payment you can use to pay for your charges in Microsoft Advertising. | Follow the steps in [this help article](hlp_BA_CONC_AvailablePaymentMethods.md) to add a payment method. |

## How to use the diagnostic tile

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Campaigns** and select **Campaigns**.
1. Select a campaign.
1. Select **Overview**.
1. Locate the **Diagnostics tile**.
1. Review the health statuses and messages.
1. Select the suggested actions (e.g., **Fix ads**, **Manage ads**) to resolve the issues.
