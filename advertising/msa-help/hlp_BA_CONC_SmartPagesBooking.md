# How to book appointments through your smart page

> [!NOTE]
> Microsoft Smart Page will no longer be available after **May 31, 2026**. After this date, you won't be able to access existing Smart Pages or create new ones. [Learn more](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPages.md)

In your smart page, customers can book appointments for your business. Learn how to set up, modify, and delete bookings in your smart page below.

## Set up appointments in your smart page

To accept bookings from your customers, follow these steps:

1. Sign into Smart Pages.
1. Optionally, add a **Services** section to your Smart Page to describe what booking services your business offers.
1. Change your **Customer action button** to **Book appointment**.
1. Add the email address you'd like customers to use to contact you.
1. Select **When can customers book appointments with you?** to set your available booking hours.

   > [!NOTE]
   > Currently, customers can't book multiple appointments at overlapping times.
1. Optionally, if you'd like to manage your bookings through an external website, select **Use an external booking website** and include the website you'll use for bookings.
1. In the upper-right corner, select **Publish** to publish your changes online. If you make any changes to your booking details in the future, you'll need to publish your website for those changes to reflect on your published Smart Page.

> [!NOTE]
> - You can [edit your website](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPageEdit.md) at any time.
> - Optimize your website to attract more customers. [Learn best practices to optimize your website](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPageOptimization.md).
> - From time to time, we may update the design of your website to optimize it and help customers connect more easily with your business. The content of the website components you've edited won't be modified.

## Manage existing bookings

Once a customer books an appointment with you, you'll receive an email confirmation at the address you provided in the **Customer action button** section. You'll also receive an email at the start of each week with all of your scheduled appointments for that week. To view and manage your bookings, you can log into your [Smart Pages homepage](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2206617) or manage your bookings from the Smart Pages editor using the **Manage existing appointments** option within your **Customer action button** section.

- If you need to make any changes to an existing appointment, we strongly recommend emailing your customer to let them know.
- To edit an appointment's length or timing, select **Edit appointment** for the appointment you want to change, make any necessary changes, and then select **Update appointment**.
- If you need to cancel an appointment, select **Cancel appointment** and confirm that you want to cancel on the next screen.
- For anything else, please email your customer directly using the email address they've provided.
