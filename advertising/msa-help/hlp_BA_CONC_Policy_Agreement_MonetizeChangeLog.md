# Microsoft Monetize Advertising agreement Change log

## March 2026

### Payments.

We clarified Microsoft’s position around not paying for invalid traffic.

## December 2025

### Content.

We clarified the rights you grant to us for the use of your Content.

## August 2025

- **Preamble.**

  We clarified that the English version of the Microsoft Monetize Advertising Agreement prevails over any translated version.

- **Section 4—Payments.**

  We updated the payment terms to account for when you pay us and to clarify tax requirements for Australia.

- **Exhibit A.**

  We made the following updates to Exhibit A:

  - **Definitions—Managed Impressions.** We clarified the definition of managed impressions regarding the ad server service.
  - **Technical Support.** The technical support provision was deleted to remove the limitation around the submission of support tickets. Additionally, we have clarified certain standard disclosures.
  - **Data Marketplace Terms.** The data marketplace is not currently offered to Monetize users and so we deleted references to this service.
  - **Curation Auction Services Terms.** We clarified the terms that are applicable to this service.
  - **Prebid Server Premium Terms.** We clarified publishers’ responsibility regarding data processing and demand partners.

- **Exhibit B—Section 1.**

  We made the following updates to Exhibit B:

  - Tiered pricing is not available and therefore all references were deleted.
  - We clarified the fees applicable for certain activities, including tracker fees, creative audit fees, creative hosting fee, and creative overage fee.
