# Search terms insights

Search terms insights allow you to pinpoint high-performing search terms and enhance your campaign's performance based on customer search behavior. These terms are sorted into search categories, each providing key performance metrics. Search categories are auto-generated groupings of search terms that include all search terms, even those not shown in the search terms report. By tracking these metrics, you can assess the effectiveness of your campaigns and asset groups, identifying which terms are driving traffic.

> [!NOTE]
> Search terms insights are only available for Performance Max campaigns and it can take up to three days to see results in the Search terms insights report.

## Benefits of using Search terms insights

- **Quickly understand customer intent:** Gain an understanding of what your target customers are searching for using auto-generated search categories without having to sift through individual search terms in the search terms report.
- **Enhance performance with tailored recommendations:** Act on recommendations that help tailor your creatives and landing pages to incorporate top search categories, helping to boost performance.
- **Identify and capture demand:** Identify which campaigns and ad groups are capturing the most demand based on the search categories.

## Search categories

Search categories are organized based on what customers are searching for and the characteristics of your products or services. For each search category, you can view which campaigns and ad groups had the most traffic and review performance metrics for:

- Clicks
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversions
- Conversion rate
- Conversion value
- Impressions
- Search volume

## Run a search terms insights report

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Reporting** > **Performance** > **Search terms insights**.
1. Make any desired changes to the report's conditional formatting, date/time range, account scope, or columns.
