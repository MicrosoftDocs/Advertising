<a id="set-up-conversion-tracking-for-online-activities"></a>

## Set up conversion tracking for online activities

<a id="automatic-tracking"></a>

### Automatic tracking

The UET tag will automatically detect events on your websites, such as page visits and button clicks, and can categorize those events into relevant conversion goals. This eliminates the need for manually configuring conversion goals.
