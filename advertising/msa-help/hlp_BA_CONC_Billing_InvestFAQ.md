# Microsoft Advertising contract cut-over FAQ

## Invest impact overview

If you were previously a Xandr customer who has recently signed a new agreement with Microsoft Advertising, please note that a few things are changing for you:

**1. Invoice/Payment Impact Prior to Microsoft contract effective date**

You will be receiving the final Xandr invoice for Invest service **up to the effective date** of the new Microsoft Advertising contract agreement in the same manner, timing, as it has been. Please pay the final Xandr bill into the same bank account that you typically send to, as reflected in Xandr invoice.

**2. Invoice/Payment Impact After Microsoft contract effective date**

For Invest services **after the effective date** of the new Microsoft Advertising contract, you will be receiving a **new Microsoft Advertising invoice** which *may have* different net terms and invoice currency than what you have previously had under the prior contract. Additionally, you will notice that Microsoft Advertising invoices have a different bank account under the payment instruction section.

You may need to add Microsoft Advertising as a new supplier with the new bank account details as it appears on the Microsoft Advertising invoice in your account payable system/tool.

It is important that you pay the invoices in a timely manner and in accordance with the payment instructions on the invoices to reduce the potential risk of service disruptions and delays in reflecting the payments on your accounts.

**3. Where to find your Microsoft Advertising invoice?**

Your Invest invoice will still be available to access on the Microsoft Invest platform. If you are a prior customer that is also buying Microsoft Advertising services, your Microsoft Advertising invoice will be available on the Microsoft Advertising Platform.

Thank you for becoming a Microsoft Advertising customer!
