# Change contact information

By keeping your contact information current, you make it easier for Microsoft Advertising team members to contact you when it is important to do so. It is your responsibility to maintain this information so that you can continue to receive important notifications in a timely manner.

## Change your contact information

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Settings** and select **My settings**.
1. In the **Contact information** section, you can change your name, email address, phone numbers, etc.
1. Select **Save**.

## Change your legal business name

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Account settings**
1. Under **Account information**, select the pencil icon ![pencil icon](media/BA_icon_edit.png) to edit your account information.
1. To change your **Legal business name**, expand the **Business address** dropdown and select **Enter a new address**. A new field for **Legal business name** will appear.
1. Enter your **Legal business name** and the associated address.
1. Select **Save**.

To change or manage your business addresses, please visit [Managing your addresses](hlp_BA_PROC_ManagingAddresses.md).
