# Get to know your smart pages custom domains

> [!NOTE]
> Microsoft Smart Page will no longer be available after **May 31, 2026**. After this date, you won't be able to access existing Smart Pages or create new ones. [Learn more](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPages.md)

With smart pages, you can now create custom domains for your business. Custom domains allow you fine-tuned control over how your business appears to potential customers. By creating a unique custom domain, you allow customers to share your URL more easily.

You have two ways to create your personalized custom domain: Create your **smart pages** web address to a free URL that we'll provide. Connect your **M365 for Business** account to use a domain you've purchased through that account.

> [!NOTE]
> - If you haven't built your website yet, make sure you [build it first](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPages.md).
> - You can [edit your website](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPageEdit.md) at any time.
> - Optimize your website to attract more customers. [Learn best practices to optimize your website](hlp_BA_CONC_SmartPageOptimization.md).
> - If you don't already have an M365 Business account, sign up for one [here](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2225023). If you already have an M365 Business account but haven't bought any domains, you can do so [here](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2225219) once you've logged in.

## Change your custom domains through Smart Pages

1. Sign in to your **Smart Pages** account.
1. Select **View, edit, and publish my Smart Page**.
1. Select the pencil icon next to your Smart Page URL, or go to the editor's **Settings** page and select **Edit URL**.

   - If you have an M365 for Business account, select **Connect your M365 for Business** account to use a domain you've purchased. From here, you can sign into your **M365 for Business** account and select any active domain you've purchased through your M365 plan.
   - If you don't have an M365 for Business account, or don't want to use a URL you've purchased through **M365 for Business**, select **Claim your free Smart Pages URL** and enter a new URL for your Smart Page.
1. Select **Confirm your changes**.
1. If you're using your Smart Page as part of any ad campaigns, we'll automatically update your campaigns with your new domain.

   > [!NOTE]
   > - This could take up to 72 hours.
   > - Please wait at least 24 hours before changing your Smart Pages URL again.
