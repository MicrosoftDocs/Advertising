# Free coaching to help you get started

Our expert coaches are standing by to help plan, create, and improve your ads. Your coach will help you:

- Set up your Microsoft Advertising account.
- Import your ads from your current Google Ads, Meta_Ads, Pinterest_Ads account.
- Create your Search ads, Display and Native ads, Video ads, and Shopping campaigns.
- Create keywords with competitive bids and customized ads that follow [Microsoft Advertising policies](hlp_BA_CONC_EditorialGuidelines.md) and Microsoft Advertising policies.
- Track your conversions.
- Improve your ads with Microsoft Advertising reports.
- Introduce you to tools, events, and resources that will help you successfully manage your ads.

Did we mention this is free? No cost, no commitment. Just a way to help you get up and running with Microsoft Advertising. Whether you want to schedule an appointment, call us or chat with us, [contact an expert](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398320) today.
