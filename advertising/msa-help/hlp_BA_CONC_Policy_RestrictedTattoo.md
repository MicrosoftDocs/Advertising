# Tattoos and body piercings

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to all ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 8, 2026.

Advertising for business services of tattoos, body branding, body piercing, tongue-splitting, or other methods of legal body modification, must be targeted to 18+.

Images showing tattoos or body piercings must also comply with the [Offensive, Harmful, or Violent Content Policy](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedOffensive.md).

## What Happens When an Ad Policy is Violated

The penalty varies by the nature of the violation, and the number of violations. [Learn More](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_ImpactToYourMicrosoftAdsAccount.md)
