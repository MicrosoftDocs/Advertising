# Microsoft Curate Advertising agreement Change log

## March 2026

### Payments.

We clarified Microsoft’s position around not paying for invalid traffic.

## December 2025

### Content.

We clarified the rights you grant to us for the use of your Content.

## August 2025

- **Preamble.**

  We clarified that the English version of the Microsoft Curate Advertising Agreement prevails over any translated version. We also included a term to account for use of your Curate account as an agent on behalf of your customer.

- **Section 4—Payments.**

  We updated the payment terms to account for when you pay us and to clarify tax requirements for Brazil and Australia.

- **Exhibit B.**

  We made the following updates to Exhibit B:

  - **Curation Auction Service Charge.** Renamed “Curation Action Service Charge” to “Broker Auction Service Charge” to align with the terminology on invoice reporting.
  - **Tiered Pricing.** We updated the tiered pricing description to include Margin Auction Service Charges.
  - **Data Marketplace Fees.** We updated this section to reflect how we charge data marketplace fees.
  - **Taxes.** Minor changes to reflect updated tax terms.
