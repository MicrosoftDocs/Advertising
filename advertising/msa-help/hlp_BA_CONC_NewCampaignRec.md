# New campaign recommendations

> [!NOTE]
> This feature is currently available for campaigns that use the following [auto-bidding strategies](hlp_BA_CONC_BidStrategy.md): Maximize conversions and Maximize conversion value.

When you create a new campaign in Microsoft Advertising, you’ll receive budget recommendations and performance estimates to help you start with confidence. These suggestions use historical data from similar campaigns so you can make informed choices without relying on guesswork.

For example, during the setup of a Performance Max campaign, you might see a recommended budget of $20/day with an estimated 45 conversions and 1,200 clicks over the next 30 days. These insights appear when you set your budget and bidding strategy and help guide you toward an investment that supports your campaign goals.

## Budget options

If you're using automated bidding, you'll see one of the following budget options—each designed to support different campaign goals and learning stages. Options include estimated outcomes and metrics such as clicks, conversions, impressions, and click-through rate (CTR):

- **Starter**: Minimum budget to gather data and begin learning.
- **Balanced**: Moderate budget to efficiently reach your performance goals.
- **Advanced**: Higher budget to maximize results quickly.

> [!NOTE]
> You can use these recommendations as guidance when choosing a budget and bidding strategy; you can always adjust settings later based on real campaign performance.
