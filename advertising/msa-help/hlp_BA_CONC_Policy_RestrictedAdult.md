# Adult content

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to Search. Adult is not allowed in other ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 8, 2026.

At its sole discretion and at any time, Microsoft reserves the right to define and amend what is considered adult content, amend the published adult policy, restrict the presence of adult content across the network, and disable the use of adult keywords.

The Adult Advertising Program is only available in the following markets: Albania, Australia, Belgium, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Canada, Croatia, Czech Republic, Estonia, France, Germany, Greece, Iceland, Italy, Montenegro, Netherlands, New Zealand, Republic of Ireland, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, South Africa, Spain, United Kingdom, United States, and Taiwan.

> [!NOTE]
> Ads must target only markets that are allowed in the Adult Advertising Program. If Microsoft does not allow Adult advertising in a certain market, your ad is prohibited there.

- **Definition**

  Microsoft classifies advertising as adult content if the ad copy or the target website features or promotes sexually explicit content including, but not limited to:

  - Pornography.
  - Dating services with clear intent for sexual encounters, where the website content features graphic images on the landing page, or the language is graphic or explicit in nature.
  - Content promoting Adult entertainment and related brands.
  - Explicit lingerie that exposes intimate body areas.
  - Risqué content which could include:

    - Language that refers to the act of sex for pleasure.
    - Content that is intended to titillate the user to think about sex or refers to products that aid in the act of sex.
    - Content or imagery that suggests sexualization of an ordinary object.

- **Disallowed adult content**

  Microsoft does not accept ads for the following:

  - Illegal, non-consensual, denigrating, obscene, or violent activity, including bestiality, brutality, torture, death, illegal drug use, cruelty, pedophilia, rape, incest, extreme or shocking sex, or child sexual exploitation and abuse (CSEA) or child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  - Strippers/strip clubs, exotic dancers, or “gentlemen’s” clubs.
  - Paid or compensated companionship, dating, or sex acts such as escorts, sex work,“sugar” relationships, or cuddle services.
  - Sites that have content showing people who are, or are portrayed to be, under the legal age permitted in applicable jurisdiction.

  Ads must not contain content that is offensive or contrary to public morals in any of the markets that you target, as applicable.

  Please also see [Offensive, Harmful, and Violent Content](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedOffensive.md), [Trafficking and Exploitation](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedTrafficking.md), and[Umoderated User-Generated Content](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedUnregulated.md).

- **Adult advertising policies**

  At its sole discretion and at any time, Microsoft reserves the right to define and amend what is considered adult content, amend the published adult policy, restrict the presence of adult content across the network, and disable the use of adult keywords.

  - Ads for sites that contain sexually explicit content must contain language that clearly indicates that the site contains sexually explicit content. Space permitting, your ads must indicate that the landing page is for ages 18+ or for adults only.
  - Sites that do not feature sexually explicit content on the landing page or as a dominant theme on the website, but that link to adult content, must employ a “bridge page” warning users that adult content will be found when the link is clicked.

  The landing page must be a bridge page when linking to adult content. Additional bridge page requirements may exist for some markets; please see the market-specific country restrictions below for additional information

  :::image type="content" source="media/Policies_CreativeSpecs8.png" alt-text="Landing page must be a bridge page when linking to adult content.":::

  :::image type="content" source="media/Policies_CreativeSpecs9.png" alt-text="Landing pages may not link directly to adult content without the use of a bridge page.":::

  - Sites that feature adult images must include an age disclaimer on the site and all models must have the appearance of being 18 years or older.

    - Examples of acceptable age disclaimers include, but are not limited to, 18 U.S.C. Section 2257 Compliance Notice and 18 U.S.C. 2257 Record Keeping Requirements Compliance Statement for the U.S.
    - This disclaimer must indicate that all models are 18 years or older and link to the Custodian of Records page.
    - Ads, keywords, landing pages, and website content must comply with all other Microsoft Advertising policies.

- **Adult advertising participation**

  Advertisers must apply to the Adult Advertising Program and must comply with applicable regulations and all Microsoft policies. Adult ads are allowed only in text Search ads.

  If you would like to apply to the program, complete and submit the [Adult Advertising Participation Form](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2274132). Your application will be considered separately for each country in which you want your advertisements to appear.

- **As a participant in the program**

  - You must use age targeting for 18+, or the age of majority in the market in which you are targeting, whichever is the higher age.
  - You are responsible for all content that is related to or accessible from your site, including, but not limited to, links, text, audio, visual, or image content, as well as content linked to from the site even if it is provided by a third party.
  - You must comply with all applicable industry regulations, standards of practice, and legal requirements including, but not limited to, those concerning age verification of models featured on the site.
  - You agree to give Microsoft immediate, unrestricted, and unconditional access to all sites that are associated with adult ads, at no charge, in cases where we need to investigate compliance with our Microsoft Advertising policies.
  - For more information and the approved adult keyword list, please [Contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2274132) or reach out to your account manager.

- **Adult keywords and advertising**

  - Keywords that are approved for adult advertising content must be solely and explicitly for adult content. For example, Bing users searching approved adult keywords are only served sexually explicit content, while non-adult-specific keywords do not serve adult content.

    - Examples of approved adult keywords include: Free Porn, XXX Video, Live Sex Chat Cams, Phone Sex Lines, Sex Toys, Blow up Dolls or Rabbit Vibrator.
  - Keywords that are not explicitly related to adult content — keywords that are relevant to both an adult search results category and a non-adult search results category — are not allowed.

    - Examples of keywords that are not explicitly related to adult content include: Breast Enhancement, Condoms, Lubricants, Lingerie, Sex Education or Single Dating.
  - You may only bid on adult keywords from our approved adult keyword list for your adult campaign, only using the “Exact” match type. Bidding on phrase or broad match types will prevent your ads from delivering.

- **Display/Native/Audiences/Product/Video/CTV**

  Adult advertising is prohibited.
