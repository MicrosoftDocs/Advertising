# How call and location extensions work together

> [!NOTE]
> - **Call extensions are available to set up in:** [All Microsoft Advertising markets](hlp_BA_CONC_Countries.md). **Call extensions serve in**: Available in Australia, the United Kingdom, and the United States on all devices. Available in all other [Microsoft Advertising markets](hlp_BA_CONC_Countries.md) on smartphones only, except for Belgium and China.
> - **Location extensions are available to set up and serve in:** [All Microsoft Advertising markets](hlp_BA_CONC_Countries.md), except Belgium and China.

[Call extensions,](hlp_BA_PROC_AddCallExtension.md) can be used together with [location extensions](hlp_BA_PROC_AddLocationExtension.md). When these two features are used at the same time, the phone number displayed (coming from one or the other extension) and the availability of tap-to-call depend on the device where the ad is being viewed. The table below describes what you can expect.

| Options you've selected | What's displayed on<br>a computer or tablet | What's displayed on<br>a smartphone |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Location extension that includes a phone number<br>(Call extension not enabled)** | Location extension phone number | Location extension phone number<br>(tap-to-call) |
| **Call extension: Show my number only on mobile phones<br>(Location extension not enabled)** | N/A (No phone numbers) | Call extension phone number<br>(tap-to-call) |
| **Call extension: Show a Microsoft Advertising forwarding number<br>(Location extension not enabled)** | Bing and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number (tap-to-call)<br><br>AOL and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number (tap-to-call)<br><br>Yahoo and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number | Call extension forwarding phone number<br>(tap-to-call) |
| **Location extension that includes a phone number<br>and<br>Call extension: Show my number only on mobile phones** | Location extension phone number | Location extension phone number<br>(tap-to-call) |
| **Location extension that includes a phone number<br>and<br>Call extension: Show a Microsoft Advertising forwarding number** | Location extension phone number<br>and<br>Bing and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number (tap-to-call)<br><br>AOL and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number (tap-to-call)<br><br>Yahoo and syndicated partner sites and networks: Call extension forwarding phone number | Location extension phone number (tap-to-call) |

To learn about call, location, and other ad extensions, head over to [What are ad extensions](hlp_BA_CONC_AboutAdExtensions.md).
