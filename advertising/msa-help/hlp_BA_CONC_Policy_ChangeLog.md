# Microsoft Advertising policies Change log

Learn what changes have occurred in the Microsoft Advertising policies over time.

- **May 2026**

  [Gambling and Games](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedGambling.md)

  Taiwan

  The Gambling and Games policy has been updated to allow ads for social and sweepstakes casinos in Taiwan.

- **May 2026**

  [Pilot Program Policies](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_PilotPrograms.md)

  United States

  Predictions sites registered with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) as a Designated Contract Market (DCM) are now eligible to run ads in select states as part of a pilot program.

- **May 2026**

  [Gambling and Games](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedGambling.md)

  Belgium

  The Gambling and Games policy has been updated to allow ads for casinos, sports betting, and fantasy sports on Search.

- **April 2026**

  [Trafficking or Exploitation](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_DisallowedTrafficking.md)

  Global

  The Trafficking and Exploitation policy updates include language about exploitative business models, transactional international matchmaking, and coercive relationship models.

- **February 2026**

  [Dating and Companionship](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedDating.md)

  Global

  The Dating and Companionship policy updates include additional general requirements and a new pre-approval requirement.

- **February 2026**

  [Dating and Companionship](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedDating.md)

  Japan

  The Dating and Companionship policy updates to include market requirements for advertisers targeting Japan.

- **February 2026**

  [Dating and Companionship](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedDating.md)

  United States

  The Dating and Companionship policy updates to include market requirements for advertisers targeting Colorado.

- **January 2026**

  [Gambling and Games](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedGambling.md)

  Global

  The policies have been updated to define games of skill, games of chance, and social “sweepstakes” casinos, and expand to our [Pre-Approval Process](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2262174) to all gambling and games clients.

- **January 2026**

  [Pharmacy and Healthcare](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedPharma.md)

  Global

  Advertisers who wish to run ads for online pharmacies or prescription drugs must go though our [Pre-Approval Process](https://about.ads.microsoft.com/en/forms/pharmacy-and-telemedicine-advertising-program) prior to serving in allowed markets.

- **January 2026**

  [Finance](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedFinance.md)

  Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czechia, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, and United Kingdom.

  The policies have been updated to allow Cryptocurrency exchanges for Search advertisers who meet certain criteria in these markets.

- **January 2026**

  [Food](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedFood.md)

  United Kingdom

  The policies have been updated to disallow high fat, sugar, and salt foods in the United Kingdom starting January 5, 2026.

- **December 2025**

  [Nudity and Suggestive Content](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_RestrictedNudityandSuggestive.md)

  Global

  Nudity and Suggestive Content policy moved from Media Formats policy to a standalone restricted content policy.

- **December 2025**

  [Cryptocurrency](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_PilotPrograms.md)

  India, Japan

  The policies have been updated to allow for cryptocurrency wallet advertisers who meet certain criteria in India and Japan.

- **December 2025**

  [Finance](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_PilotPrograms.md)

  Germany, United Kingdom

  The policies have been updated to allow contracts for difference (CFD) advertisers who meet certain criteria in Germany and the United Kingdom.

- **December 2025**

  [Gambling](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_PilotPrograms.md)

  United States

  The policies have been updated to allow managed affiliate advertisers to participate in our sports betting and casino pilots.

- **September 2025**

  [Gambling](hlp_BA_CONC_Policy_PilotPrograms.md)

- **August 2025**
