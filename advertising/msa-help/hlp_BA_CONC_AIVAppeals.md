# Advertiser identity verification appeals

Microsoft Advertising requires advertiser identity verification (AIV) to ensure that customers see ads from trusted sources to improve their overall experience.

You'll have three attempts to complete your identity verification. If your application fails, you can either resubmit once you review the reasons for the failure and attempt to fix it, or appeal the failed decision if you feel it was incorrect. However, if an appeal is submitted, you cannot make a new AIV attempt until the appeal is reviewed, which ideally takes 7-10 business days. If all three attempts fail and you have any issues or concerns with your verification results, you can submit an appeal or contact [Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) for further assistance.

Before submitting an appeal, make sure to review the documents carefully, as you're only allowed to submit an appeal once.

Depending on your AIV failure type, you can choose to appeal as a business or individual.

- You can choose to appeal as a business if you represent or promote a business; for example, a law firm or a plumbing service.
- You can choose to appeal as an individual if you represent or promote yourself as an individual; for example, if you're a freelance writer or food truck owner. You may also need to appeal as an individual if you provide an email outside of your business domain.

> [!NOTE]
> The documentation you need to provide will differ based on your location. [Learn more about the advertiser identity verification (AIV) process](hlp_BA_CONC_AdvertiserIdentityVerification.md).

- **To submit an AIV appeal, please follow these steps**

  1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Settings** and select **Account verifications**.

     > [!NOTE]
     > If you have multiple accounts, choose one from the account dropdown menu on the top left.
  1. If you have tried AIV and failed, you should see a blue popup with an appeal request form link under the **Advertiser identity verification (AIV) status** section.
  1. Select the link and fill out the form to submit an AIV appeal.

> [!NOTE]
> To learn more about the advertiser identity appeals process, please contact your account manager or [Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371).
