# Pinned call extensions for Vertical ads

Drive more leads with pinned call extensions for [Vertical ads](hlp_BA_CONC_AboutVerticalAds.md), which help you reach your target audience with more engaging ads.

## The benefits of using pinned call extensions

- **Engage a high-intent audience.** Customers who call a business are ten times more likely to purchase than those who don't.
- **Encourage customer engagement.** Simplify your customers' process of making a call to your business.

## How it works

When a customer clicks on a pinned call extension, they're directly led to a call option (on phones) or a QR code they can use to call your business (on computers). You may either use your own phone number or, for tracking purposes, a Microsoft Advertising forwarding phone number.

A Microsoft Advertising forwarding phone number is a unique phone number that's routed to your business's phone number. With a Microsoft Advertising forwarding number, you can track all calls from your ad so that you can analyze the ad's performance.

- To get a Microsoft Advertising forwarding number, an ad group within a campaign using call extensions must have at least ten clicks and $10.00 in spend in the last 30 days.

  > [!NOTE]
  > If this condition isn't met, the number used won't be a Microsoft Advertising forwarding number.
- If a forwarding number is used, you can access reports that provide details like call duration and missed calls on all calls your ads have generated.

Any calls you receive from ads that are clicked are charged per click, similar to regular Search ads.

## Get started

> [!NOTE]
> Pinned call extensions are not available for Property Promotion ads.

You can enter one call extension per campaign.

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Campaigns** and select **Ads & extensions** > **Extensions**.
1. Select the campaign for which you’d like to create a pinned call extension.
1. Select **Create**.
1. Enter an eligible phone number.
1. Under **Mobile format**, select **Show both my website and the phone number**.

   > [!NOTE]
   > Choosing the other option will cause the pinned call extension to experience loading issues.
1. Select **Save**.
