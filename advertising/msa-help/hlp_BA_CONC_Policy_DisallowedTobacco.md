# Tobacco and vaping products

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to all ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 8, 2026.

Microsoft commits to safeguard individuals and communities from potential risks and health hazards. Advertising for the following products or services is prohibited:

- Advertising for the sale or use of cigarettes, cigars, smokeless tobacco, cigarette papers, blunt wraps, or any other preparation of tobacco or nicotine. This includes cigar bars, hookah lounges, and any businesses where the primary focus is facilitating smoking.
- Any instrument or paraphernalia that is designed for the smoking or ingestion of products containing tobacco or nicotine.
- Advertising for electronic cigarettes or vape products, including the cartridges and liquid solutions that are used in such devices, even those containing no nicotine.

Advertising generally is allowed for products and services that are clearly intended for smoking cessation as long as they are not consumed or otherwise used in the same way as the tobacco/nicotine product they are replacing.

Advertising is generally allowed for ashtrays unless smoking is depicted in the ad.
