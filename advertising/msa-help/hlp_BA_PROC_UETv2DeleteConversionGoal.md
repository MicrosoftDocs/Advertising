# How do I delete a conversion goal?

Managing conversion tracking can get complicated as your account grows. Many advertisers reach the limit on conversion goals or accumulate unused tags, making it harder to keep accounts organized and campaigns running efficiently.

You can simplify and manage your setup by deleting UET tags. When you delete a tag, any associated conversion goals are also deleted. Currently, only smart goals and goals created using the simplified setup method can be deleted, by deleting the associated UET tags.

You can delete a UET tag unless it's required for campaign-level conversion tracking. Depending on its status, deletion may remove goals, stop tracking, and affect performance.

## Delete a UET tag and its associated goals

1. Go to conversion goals grid, find the tag ID column for the conversion goal you want to delete and note the tag ID associated with that goal.
1. In the **All tags** grid on the UET tag page, select the checkbox next to the tag or tags.
1. In the blue banner, select **Delete**.
1. Confirm the warning message.

If the tag is deleted successfully, the associated goals are also removed. If the deletion fails, an error table appears at the top of the page. Follow the instructions shown to resolve the issue, or choose to delete the tag anyway to remove the associated goals.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Deleting goals or tags can impact campaign performance, especially if those conversions were used for optimization.
