# Critical events

> [!NOTE]
> To ensure consistency worldwide, Microsoft Advertising enforces our policies across our ad network using the English version. We provide translated versions as a courtesy to our clients, though the translations are not meant to change the content or meaning of the official policy language.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This policy applies to all ad types.

> [!NOTE]
> This page was last updated on April 8, 2026.

Microsoft Advertising does not want to exploit or profit from unexpected high-impact events. As a result, Microsoft may remove or limit advertising in response to a tragedy, natural disaster, public emergency, death, or high-profile news event such as an act of terrorism or war, particularly if advertising:

- May appear to exploit events for commercial gain.
- May be considered inappropriate, especially given certain events or circumstances.
- May affect user safety (for example a product recall).

Restrictions may include prohibiting:

- Use of key words related to the sensitive event.
- News article content advertising related to the event.
