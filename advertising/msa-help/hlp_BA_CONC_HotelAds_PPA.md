# About Property Promotion ads

> [!NOTE]
> - Learn more about where [Property Promotion ads are available](hlp_BA_CONC_Countries.md).
> - These ads are currently available globally in English. If you'd like to serve them in another language, don't worry—more languages are coming soon!

Property Promotion ads (PPA) are designed to inspire travelers who are looking for [hotels or vacation rentals](hlp_BA_CONC_HotelAds_LodgingCategories.md) early in the planning process, when you can influence their decision. These placements increase the visibility of your properties and drive more bookings. PPA surface across the Microsoft Advertising Network in both search and audience placements when someone searches for properties in a specific location or with certain amenities.

## The benefits of Property Promotion ads

- **Drive bookings.** Get brand exposure with strong intent matching and rich visuals that lead to high engagement and improved click-through rates.
- **Inspire and engage travelers.** Entice travel planners with a rich, engaging, and curated experience, complete with callouts and images you have full control over.
- **Save time with easy setup.** Power your ads with familiar feeds and syntaxes, and ad management workflows with built-in reporting, budgeting, and bid multiplying capabilities.

## Get started with Property Promotion ads

Before you can get started with PPA, you'll need to set up [Hotel Price ads](hlp_BA_CONC_HotelAds_Intro.md).

Please be prepared to provide at least one image for each property or five images for Bing Maps placements. These should be high-quality images sized at 1200 x 628 pixels. Optionally, you can include a star rating in your Property feed. For more detailed information about Property feeds, please see [Create an XML Hotel Feed file](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2255125).

1. Create a [Lodging campaign](hlp_BA_CONC_HotelCampaigns.md).
1. Make sure to select the **Property Promotion ad (PPA)** checkbox under **Hotel ad format**.
1. Under **Site type**, set a bid for **Property Promotion Ads** by choosing one of the following options from the dropdown menu:

   - **Increase by** and enter 1000%.
   - **Decrease by** and enter 100%.
1. Select **Save**.

> [!NOTE]
> - To opt out of PPA, select **Decrease by** and enter 100%.
> - It's important to note that after you create the ad group, you cannot change its ad format. You'd have to delete the ad group altogether. You can review the ad format of your ad group by selecting **All campaigns** > **Ad groups** > **Ad groups**. In the table, select the ad group whose ad format you want to check. From the left pane, select **Settings**.
