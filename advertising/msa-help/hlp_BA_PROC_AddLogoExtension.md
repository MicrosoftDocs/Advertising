# Logo extensions: Add a logo to your ad

> [!NOTE]
> - **Starting on March 17, 2025, we'll automatically add one account-level logo extension to new and existing accounts.** This helps promote your brand and build brand recognition.
> - You may adjust and/or delete this logo extension, and we won't create it again for your account.
> - Your account is ineligible for this automatic change if you're an advertiser for pharmaceuticals, financial services, or adult content. Your account will also be ineligible if you've opted out of [automated business logo extensions](hlp_BA_CONC_AutomatedExtensions.md), or if you already added logo extensions at the account, campaign, or ad group levels.

Promote your brand and stand out from the competition by displaying eye-catching logos in your ads. Logo extensions help you build brand recognition to drive more clicks and conversions on your website. Logo extensions serve on Bing across computer, tablet, and mobile devices and can appear with other extensions. You can associate your logo extensions at the account, campaign, or ad group levels, but only one extension is allowed per level. Please note we'll prioritize logo extensions during ad delivery over any [automated business logo extensions](hlp_BA_CONC_AutomatedExtensions.md) you have set up.

> [!NOTE]
> For [Performance Max campaigns](hlp_BA_PROC_CreatePmax.md) that have logos in an asset group and a logo extension at the account level, the asset group's logo will take priority during ad delivery.

**Ability to set up in:** All Bing markets

**Serves in**: Bing owned and operated traffic globally on computers and Copilot in Bing

## Requirements

You can add one square logo per account, campaign, or ad group. Your logos must meet the following requirements:

- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1
- **Accepted file types**: PNG and JPG
- **Maximum file size**: 5120 KB
- **Minimum resolution**: 128 x 128 pixels (recommended 128 x 128 pixels)

## Create a logo extension

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Campaigns** and select **Ads & extensions** > **Extensions**.
1. Select **Account**, **Campaign**, or **Ad group** to associate your extension at one of these levels.
1. From the extensions dropdown, select **Logo extensions**.
1. Select **Create** > **Add new Logo extension**.
1. Add your **Business logo**.
1. Enter your **Business name**.
1. Enter the **Domain name** for your business website. For example: “contoso.com”.

   > [!NOTE]
   > Your domain name shouldn't include any subdirectories.
1. Select **Save**.
1. You'll see the new extension added to the **Selected logo extensions** list. On the main extension page, select **Save** to finish adding the extension to your account, campaign, or ad group.

Go to the [About ad extensions](hlp_BA_CONC_AboutAdExtensions.md) page for instructions to:

- Add, edit, and delete ad extensions
- Associating ad extensions with an account or campaign

> [!NOTE]
> Standard performance reports are available for all extensions. Learn how to [create a report](hlp_BA_PROC_CreateReport.md).
