# Disclaimer in ads

Disclaimer in ads allows you to inform customers about specific information related to your business or products. You can add Disclaimer in ads to new or existing campaigns.

## Get started with Disclaimer in ads

> [!NOTE]
> - Disclaimer in ads is not supported on Multimedia ads.
> - Disclaimer in ads is supported for Performance Max campaigns that don't serve [Product ads](hlp_BA_CONC_AboutProductAds.md) (i.e., campaigns without a catalog feed file).
> - If your Audience campaign is serving Display or Video ads, the disclaimer won't work; however, the respective creatives will serve with disclaimers.
> - Once you enable a disclaimer, you'll lose traffic from publishers who don't support disclaimers.

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. For Audience or Search campaigns, choose **Drive conversions**, **Generate leads**, or **Build brand awareness** as your campaign goal. For Performance Max campaigns, choose **Drive conversions** or **Sell products**.
1. Select **Audience**, **Performance Max**, or **Search** as your campaign type.

   > [!NOTE]
   > Disclaimer in ads for Audience campaigns will only work for Native ads.
1. Move through the campaign creation wizard.
1. For Audience campaigns, under **Budget & bid strategy**, select the **Show a disclaimer in Native ads for this campaign** checkbox under **Disclaimer**.
   - For Performance Max campaigns, under **Advanced campaign settings**, select the **Show a disclaimer in ads for this campaign** checkbox under **Disclaimer**.
   - For Search campaigns, under **Advanced settings** in **Campaign settings**, select the **Show a disclaimer in ads for this campaign** checkbox under **Disclaimer**.
1. Select **Add disclaimer**.
1. Follow the steps in the **Create disclaimer** window. You can choose a **Popup layout** that requires you to add a **Disclaimer title** or **Additional line layout**, which will replace Ad text 2.

   *Notes:*

   - While **Popup layout** is your only option for Audience and Performance Max campaigns, you may choose between **Popup layout** and **Additional line layout** for Search campaigns.
   - If you're creating a disclaimer for ads powered by dynamic data feeds (e.g., Automotive ads, and Credit Card ads, you *must* choose **Popup layout**.
   - Due to China's regulation requirements, Chinese advertisers can only create disclaimers using **Additional line layout**.
1. Select **Save**.
1. Choose a disclaimer to associate with your campaign.
1. Follow the steps provided by the campaign wizard. Learn more about the different steps of [the Audience campaign](hlp_BA_PROC_CreateAudienceCampaign.md) and [the Search campaign](hlp_BA_PROC_Create1stAd.md) creation wizards.
1. For Search campaigns, when you get to **Ads & ad extensions**, select **Dynamic Search ad** or **Responsive Search ad** from the **Ad type** dropdown menu.

   Also in **Ads & ad extensions**, you can preview your ad with the disclaimer by selecting **Create ad**.
1. Select **Save**.

> [!NOTE]
> While clicking on disclaimers is free, you can still see disclaimer ad performance on the ad level report.
