# Managing your India tax ID

Advertisers based in India must provide either a company or an individual Permanent Account Number (PAN) as it appears on the PAN card.

PAN is a 10-digit alphanumeric code supplied to individuals and businesses in India by the Indian Income Tax Department. New and current advertisers with a business location of India will be asked to provide their PAN. Without this number, India-based advertisers will not be able to use Microsoft Advertising.

If you have a Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN), you can provide that information, but it is optional. Similar to PAN, GSTIN is a 15-digit alphanumeric code supplied to taxpayers and businesses in India by state authorities to aid with tax collection.

Microsoft Advertising cannot provide guidance on tax. Please contact your tax advisor or local tax office if you have questions.

## Tax deducted at source

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) in Microsoft Advertising payments refers to a withholding tax that customers in India are required to deduct and pay to the government on advertising costs.

A customer that requests a TDS refund must provide the India Government Form 16A and relevant invoice information. If you have all the documents and would like to request a TDS refund, please [contact Support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371).

## Changing your Permanent Account Number and Goods and Services Tax Identification Number

You can provide your company or individual Permanent Account Number (PAN) when you sign up for Microsoft Advertising and each time you create a new account.

If you are a monthly invoice customer, please contact your account manager or [contact support](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=398371) to update your PAN information.

1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Settings** and select **Account settings**.
1. Next to the **Account settings** page title, select the pencil icon ![edit icon](media/BA_icon_edit.png).
1. Enter your **Permanent Account Number (PAN)**, located just below your business address.
1. Enter your **Goods and Services Tax Identification Number (GSTIN)** if you have one (optional).
1. Select **Save**.
