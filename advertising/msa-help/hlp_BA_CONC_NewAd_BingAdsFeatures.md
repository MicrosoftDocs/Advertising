# Microsoft Advertising features

With Microsoft Advertising, you have different tools you can use manage your campaigns. Here's a quick look at what you can do in each Microsoft Advertising application.

| Microsoft Advertising | Microsoft Advertising Editor | Bing Ads API |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **- Easily import campaigns from Google Ads to Microsoft Advertising<br>- Learn how to create great ads<br>- Advertise globally or locally<br>- Reach customers in any country or region, or within a specific distance from your business<br>- Measure campaign performance with reporting<br>- Track your online advertising budget and spend<br>- Evaluate ad and keyword performance<br>- Develop insights for optimizing campaigns<br>- Create a catalog for your products with Microsoft Merchant Center** | - Manage multiple accounts simultaneously<br>- Make multiple changes quickly with bulk editing tools<br>- Copy and paste keywords, ads, even whole campaigns from one account to another<br>- Make changes before uploading to your account<br>- Work offline<br>- Easily import campaigns from Google Ads to Microsoft Advertising | - Advertise globally or locally<br>- Reach customers in any country or region, or within a specific distance from your business<br>- Measure campaign performance with reporting<br>- Track your online advertising budget and spend<br>- Evaluate ad and keyword performance<br>- Develop insights for optimizing campaigns<br>- Create a catalog for your products with Microsoft Merchant Center |
