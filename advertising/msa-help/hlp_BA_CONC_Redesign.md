# New campaign creation experience goal selections

Microsoft Advertising has a redesigned layout for creating campaigns so you can quickly and easily get your ad campaigns up and running.

To learn more about which goals you need to select to create your desired campaign and ad types, choose from the following:

- [App Install ads](#app-install-ads)
- [Audience campaigns](#audience-campaigns-native-ads-display-ads-and-video-ads) ([native ads](#native-ads), [display ads](#display-ads), and [Video ads](#video-ads))
- [Premium Streaming campaigns](#premium-streaming-campaigns)
- [Lodging campaigns](#lodging-campaigns)
- [Microsoft Store Ads](#StoreAds)
- [Performance Max campaigns](#performance-max-campaigns)
- [Search campaigns](#search-campaigns-dynamic-search-ads-multimedia-ads-responsive-search-ads-and-vertical-ads) (Dynamic Search ads, Multimedia ads, Responsive Search ads, and Vertical ads)
- [Standard Shopping campaigns](#standard-shopping-campaigns)

<a id="app-install-ads"></a>

## App Install ads

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Drive conversions**, **Generate leads**, or **Promote apps** as your campaign goal.

<a id="audience-campaigns-native-ads-display-ads-and-video-ads"></a>

## Audience campaigns (native ads, display ads, and Video ads)

<a id="native-ads"></a>

- **Native ads**

  1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
  1. Choose **Build brand awareness**, **Drive conversions**, or **Generate leads** as your campaign goal.

  <a id="display-ads"></a>

- **Display ads**

  1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
  1. Choose **Build brand awareness** as your campaign goal.

  <a id="video-ads"></a>

- **Video ads**

  1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
  1. Choose **Build brand awareness** as your campaign goal.

  <a id="premium-streaming-campaigns"></a>

## Premium Streaming campaigns

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Build brand awareness** as your campaign goal.

<a id="lodging-campaigns"></a>

## Lodging campaigns

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Drive conversions** as your campaign goal.

<a id="StoreAds"></a>

## Microsoft Store Ads

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Promote apps** as your campaign goal.

<a id="performance-max-campaigns"></a>

## Performance Max campaigns

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Drive conversions** or **Sell products** as your campaign goal.

<a id="search-campaigns-dynamic-search-ads-multimedia-ads-responsive-search-ads-and-vertical-ads"></a>

## Search campaigns (Dynamic Search ads, Multimedia ads, Responsive Search ads, and Vertical ads)

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Drive conversions** or **Generate leads** as your campaign goal.

<a id="standard-shopping-campaigns"></a>

## Standard Shopping campaigns

With standard Shopping campaigns, you can either create shopping audience or shopping Search campaigns. For more information, see [Create Product ads in a Shopping campaign](hlp_BA_PROC_CreateProductTargetAndAd.md).

1. From the navigation menu on the left, select **Create**.
1. Choose **Sell products** as your campaign goal.
