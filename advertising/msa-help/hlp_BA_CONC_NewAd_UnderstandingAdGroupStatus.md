# Understand your ad group status

Your ad group status tells you if your ad group, including all ads and keywords within that ad group, is active or not. Keep in mind that ad group status is different from campaign, ad, and keyword statuses. Ad groups fall within campaigns, and within each ad group, there are ads, keywords, and bids. Pausing an ad group also pauses all of the ads and keywords within that ad group. If you want to pause all ad groups, ads, and keywords within a campaign, simply pause the entire campaign.

**Check your ad group's status**

  1. From the navigation menu on the left, hover over **Campaigns** and select **Ad groups** > **Ad groups**.
  1. Check the statuses of individual items in the **Delivery** column.
  1. Select the **Delivery** header to sort the ad groups by status.

## What your ad group status means

### Status: Eligible

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|The ad group is set to run. | As long as your active ad content has at least one active keyword associated with it (and there are no errors with your account), your ad is available for people to see. |

### Status: Ad group expired

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|No ads are running for this ad group, ad, or keyword because the ad group’s end date has passed. | To continue running ads for the ad group, go to the **Ad groups** table, select the end date in the **End date** column, select a new end date, and then select **Save**. |

### Status: Ad group paused

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|No ads are running for this ad group, ad, or keyword because the ad group is set to pause. | To start running ads again, go to the **Ad groups** table, select the checkbox next to the appropriate ad group, and select **Edit** > **Enable**. |

### Status: Draft ad group

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|No ads are running for this ad group, ad, or keyword because the associated ad group is still in draft form. | To start running ads, go to the **Ad groups** table, select the checkbox next to the appropriate ad group, and select **Edit** > **Enable**. |

### Status: Campaign paused

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|No ads are running for this campaign, ad group, ad, or keyword because the campaign is set to **Pause**. | To start running ads again, go to the **Ad groups** table, select the checkbox next to the appropriate ad group, and select **Edit** > **Enable**. |

### Status: Campaign out of budget

| What it means | How to fix it |
| --- | --- |
|No ads are running for this campaign, ad group, ad, or keyword because your campaign budget has been used up. | To increase your budget: From the navigation menu on the left, select **Campaigns**. Go to the **Budget** column for the campaign and select the pencil icon ![pencil icon](media/BA_icon_edit.png). Increase the budget amount, and then select **Save**. |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Your account must also have no account errors or none of your ads will run, regardless of your ad group status. Find out more about [account errors](hlp_BA_CONC_AccountErrors.md).
