# AddLinkedInSegments Service Operation - Campaign Management
Adds array of LinkedIn segments to a specified campaign.

::: zone pivot="soap"

The *AddLinkedInSegmentsRequest* object defines the [body](#request-body) and [header](#request-header) elements of the service operation request. The elements must be in the same order as shown in the [Request SOAP](#request-soap).
